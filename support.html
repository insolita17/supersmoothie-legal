Support

We aim to respond to support requests within 2 business days.

Questions, bugs, account help, or anything else:

Email help.supersmoothie@gmail.com

To report community abuse, use the in-app Report action or email us with "Community abuse report" in the subject line.

Frequently asked

How do I delete my account?

Open the App, go to Profile → Privacy → Delete my account. Your account is disabled immediately and personal data is purged after the configured retention window. Some records (legal, financial, abuse-related) may be kept where required by law.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed by Apple or Google, not by us, so they must be cancelled in your platform account:

  • iPhone / iPad: Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → Super Smoothie → Cancel.
  • Android: Google Play app → tap your profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Super Smoothie → Cancel.

Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period and you keep premium access until then.

How do I get a refund?

We do not process refunds directly. Refund requests must go through the platform that billed you:

  • App Store: reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Play Store: Request a refund through Google Play

Refunds are at the sole discretion of Apple or Google under their respective policies.

How do I export my data?

In the App: Profile → Privacy → Export my data. You'll get a CSV with your account, profile, logs, and community activity.

How do I report abuse or unsafe content?

Tap the three-dot menu on any post, comment, or user and choose Report or Block. Reports go to a moderation queue and we aim to action them within 24 hours. You can also email help.supersmoothie@gmail.com with the subject line "Community abuse report".

I forgot my password.

Use the "Forgot password" link on the login screen and we will send a reset link to your registered email.

The App says my account is restricted.

Account restrictions usually follow a Community Guidelines violation. To appeal, email help.supersmoothie@gmail.com with your username and what you'd like reviewed.

How is my data used?

See our Privacy Policy for the full breakdown of what we collect, why, and how to control it.

Are smoothie scores medical advice?

No. Scores, drivers, and tips are general informational guidance, not medical, dietary, or therapeutic advice. Talk to a qualified professional for any specific health concern.
